Cheese Cake

Cheese Cake is a dessert that is made from cream cheese, eggs, and sugar. I It is baked in a water bath and then chilled. It is a very popular dessert in the United States.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Cream

Directions

  1. Mix the cheese, eggs, sugar, and cream together.
  2. Put the mixture in a water bath and bake for 30 minutes.
  3. Chill the cake for 2 hours.
  4. Serve cold.
