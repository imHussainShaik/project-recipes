Carrot Cake

Carrot Cake is a rich and moist spice cake, full of grated carrot, toasted nuts, and crushed pineapple, has great flavor, especially when covered with a tangy sweet Cream Cheese Frosting. The interesting part is that while those pretty orange flecks of grated carrot give the Carrot Cake color, sweetness, and a moist texture, its flavor is almost indistinguishable.

Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Cream
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Spices

Directions

  1. Beat the wet ingredients together, then mix in the dry ingredients. Stir in the carrots and fold in the pecans.
  2. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan or pans. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
  3. eat the frosting ingredients together until smooth and creamy. Stir in the pecans. Frost the (completely cooled) cake.
