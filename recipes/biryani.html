Recipe For Biryani

Biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan. It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped red pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped yellow pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped green beans
  • ]1/2 cup chopped carrots

Directions

  1. Boil the rice in a pot of water for 10 minutes. Drain the rice and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, saute the onion, tomato, and peppers in oil for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the green beans and carrots and saute for 5 more minutes.
  4. Add the rice and mix well.
  5. Cover the skillet and cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.
